In Duisburg, the German national team lost to North Macedonia (1: 2) in the qualifying match for the 2022 World Cup.

The last time Germany lost at home in qualifying for the 2001 World Cup was against the British (qualifying tournament for the 2002 World Cup). England in that match defeated the Germans 5-1 in Munich.

In addition, the record 18-match winning streak of the German national team in the World Cup qualification was interrupted. In the current World Cup 2022 qualifier, Germany is ranked 3rd in Group J after 3 games with 6 points.
